• Classic:Timeless and elegant with neutral tones, soft blush, and a nude or red lip.
  • Glamorous:Bold and dramatic with smoky eyes, winged eyeliner, and full lips (deep red or berry).
  • Natural: Fresh, dewy look with light coverage, neutral shades, and soft lips.
  • Elegant: Soft, feminine makeup with rosy tones and a glowing complexion.
  • Boho: Earthy, natural look with warm eyeshadow, minimal makeup
  • Modern/Edgy: Trendy, bold makeup with sharp eyeliner, contoured face, and dramatic lips.
  • Vintage: Retro glamour with winged eyeliner, red lips, and vintage-inspired hair.
  • Cultural/Traditional: Bold and ornate, often with cultural elements like intricate eyeliner or henna.
